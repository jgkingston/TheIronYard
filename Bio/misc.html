Boardgames

Boardgames can be fun, and instructional! They can also keep you out of trouble as a child because none of the popular kids will want to talk to you.

Personally I tend to find work placement games to be the most satisfying. Though tile placement and deck building games can also be fun. Dice rolling games are best enjoyed with beer.
